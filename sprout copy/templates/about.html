Our Story

Our company was started from a small coffee shop in Nashville, Tennessee. Our founders met at a local technology meetup and both shared a similar mission. One month later the company was created and we moved into our first office.

Five years later, we have now grown our company to 100 incredible employees around the globe. We still have a small office in Nashville but the majority of our employees work remote.
